Overall Happiness in The World

This dashboard is used to display how different countries rank in terms of happiness. Data for available years (2015-2020) can be selected by changing the value in the year field. Individual countries can be displayed on the line graph by clicking their location on the map or by clicking on the corresponding emoji in the scatter plot. Countries are represented on the scatter plot with an emoji that corresponds to their largest contributing factor to the overall happiness rank. In the line graph, each happiness factor can be chosen with the Y-Axis selector and its history will be displayed.

What is this Viz?

A country's measured happiness is determined through a calculation of 6 attributes that are rated from 0 to 10:

  • 🤑 GDP - A country's measured Gross Domestic Product.
  • 👨‍👩‍👦 Family/Social Support - The average family/social support system of citizens.
  • 💓 Life Expectancy - The average life expectancy for citizens.
  • 🗽 Freedom - The freedom for citizens to make their own life choices.
  • 🏛️ Trust in Government - The citizens overall faith that their government is acting in their interests.
  • 🤗 Generosity - The average amount of money a country's citizens give to charitable causes.
Each emoji on the chart below represents a country using it's highest contributing factor to their happiness.
The radial bar chart visualizes the contribution of these 6 attributes for each country's hapiness score.
